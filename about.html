About Us

Home Construction Approval was founded in 2018 with a vision to revolutionize the home construction industry by fostering better communication and collaboration among all stakeholders involved in the process.

Our founders, experienced professionals in the construction and real estate sectors, recognized the challenges faced by home buyers, builders, contractors, and inspectors due to lack of transparency and inefficient information sharing. They saw the need for a centralized platform that could bring everyone together, streamlining the construction process and ensuring that all parties were on the same page.

At Home Construction Approval, we believe that open communication and real-time updates are key to successful home construction projects. Our platform allows home buyers to stay informed about the progress of their dream home, enabling them to track the materials being used, the work completed, and the inspection status at every stage.

For builders and contractors, our platform provides a seamless way to share updates, upload documentation, and collaborate with inspectors and other professionals involved in the project. This not only enhances transparency but also helps to identify and address any potential issues early on, ensuring that projects stay on track and within budget.

Inspectors can leverage our platform to easily access relevant project information, schedule inspections, and provide detailed reports, ensuring that all work meets the required standards and codes.

At the core of our mission is a commitment to excellence and a passion for creating a better home construction experience for everyone involved. We continuously strive to improve our platform and services, incorporating feedback from our users and staying ahead of industry trends.

Join us on this journey as we redefine the way homes are built, one project at a time.
